Tower of Hanoi

Instructions

Your goal is to move all the blocks into the last column. Move the blocks by clicking on a block and then clicking on the column you want to place it in. Only one block can be moved at a time. You can only move the top block of any column.You may not place a block onto one smaller than itself.
